About ATS Resume Analyzer

ATS Resume Analyzer is an AI-powered web application designed to help students, freshers, and professionals optimize their resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). The platform analyzes resumes, matches them against job descriptions, calculates ATS scores, identifies missing skills, provides AI-driven feedback, and recommends relevant job opportunities from multiple job portals. The website is freely accessible without any login or registration, allowing users to instantly analyze their resumes and improve their chances of getting shortlisted by recruiters.

What is ATS?

ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Many companies use ATS software to filter resumes before they reach recruiters. If a resume does not contain relevant keywords or proper formatting, it may get rejected automatically.

Purpose of This Website

The main goal of this project is to help users increase their chances of getting shortlisted for jobs by improving ATS compatibility and identifying important resume improvements.

AI-Powered Analysis

The system uses AI and keyword matching techniques to analyze resumes against job descriptions, calculate match percentages, suggest improvements, and provide personalized feedback.

Why This Project Matters

Many talented candidates lose opportunities because their resumes are not optimized for ATS systems. This project bridges the gap between recruiters and job seekers by helping users create stronger, ATS-friendly resumes and improve job application success.

Tips for Better ATS Score

✅ Use keywords from the job description naturally inside your resume.
✅ Keep resume formatting simple and clean.
✅ Add relevant technical and soft skills.
✅ Avoid images, tables, and complex layouts.
✅ Update your resume according to each job role.
✅ Add measurable achievements and projects.
